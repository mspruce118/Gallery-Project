Smoky Mountain Guided Tours

Full Day Tour - 7:00 am to 3:00 pm

$400 – Price includes bottled water and sodas, snacks, sub sandwiches.
Leave from Townsend at 7:00 am and return to Townsend by 3:00 pm.
Each tour is customized for you. We depart from Townsend and drive to the chosen destinations. Some of the more elusive subjects from the animal kingdom that are in the area are Bald Eagle, Barred Owls, Black Bear Cubs, Bobcat, Bobwhite, Coyote, Elk, Hummingbirds, Northern Harrier Hawks, Pileated Woodpeckers, Osprey, and River Otter. In addition to wildlife, the Smoky Mountains offer wet and dry season waterfalls, mountain sunrises, hotspots for wildflowers, and bird nesting sites. Local knowledge on best seasons, time of day, and good angles will yield great results.
